Overview

In this assignment, you'll make your second entry into your development log, this time working on how your app's screens will look as you navigate through your app, as well as how the data flows between those screens.

Requirements

To complete this assignment, do the following:

  • READ EVERYTHING BELOW. All steps below are REQUIRED.

Screen Views

  • Sketch out designs for the screens your app will display to the user.
    • You can either do this by hand or using a program. If you do them by hand, take a picture so they can be attached to tasks in JIRA
  • The screens can include the launch screen, "Settings" and "About" pages, and everything in between.
  • For each screen sketch, make a task in JIRA and attach the image

Data flow

  • IMPORTANT (people have missed this in the past): In a separate task, define how data flows between the screens in your task. Be sure to include items like archived objects, Core Data stores, and plists. Attach a diagram to the task to show the data flow
  • Again, be as exhaustive as possible in your screen and data definitions so you have plenty of items to choose from when it comes to defining your Final Project tasks in the next log.

Note

Performing these steps NOW will make the development of you app a lot easier later. I cannot stress this enough.

Submission Requirements

You have 2 WEEKS to finish this assignment. This is so you can learn about persistence in Week 3, which you can use in describing the data flow..

There is really nothing to "submit" - just make sure your requirements are in place by the due date (Sunday of WEEK 3). NOTE: I can see the date and time that tickets were made, so I'll know if it's late!
