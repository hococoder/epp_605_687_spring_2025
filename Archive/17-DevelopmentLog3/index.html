Overview

In this assignment, you'll finish up your development log, and submit your plan for your final project.

Requirements

To complete this assignment, do the following:

  • In the previous development logs, you've laid out your requirements, screen designs, and data flow for your final project. Now, it's time to make your pitch!
  • In your JIRA console, make a new Feature Item called "Final Project", and add all of the previously defined tasks that you think you can do over the rest of the semester as SUBTASKS to this final project task. Remember this should include requirement, UI, and data flow tasks.
  • Send an email to rsteele3@jhu.edu when you've completed adding tasks, and I'll provide some feedback as to whether that will work as a final project.
  • Once you have received the OK from me, you may start working on your final project - they are due at the end of Week 13, and during week 14, you will present them to the class group. Look for more details about other steps in the final project process soon.

Submission Requirements

There is really nothing to "submit" - just make sure your requirements are in place by the due date, and EMAIL ME me when you're done!. NOTE: I can see the date and time that tickets were made, so I'll know if it's late!
